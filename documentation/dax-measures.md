# ⚙️ DAX Measures Overview

## 🧠 Purpose

This section documents the key DAX measures used in the Enterprise BI 360 dashboard. These measures form the analytical core of the solution and are reused across multiple report pages.

---

## 💰 Revenue Measures

### Net Sales
Calculates total revenue after discounts.

### Gross Sales
Total sales before any deductions.

---

## 📊 Profitability Measures

### Gross Margin
Net Sales minus Cost of Goods Sold (COGS)

### Gross Margin %
(Gross Margin / Net Sales) * 100

### Net Profit
Final profit after all operational costs

### Net Profit %
(Net Profit / Net Sales) * 100

---

## 📈 Time Intelligence Measures

### Year-over-Year (YoY) Growth
Compares current year performance with previous year.

### Month-over-Month (MoM) Growth
Compares current month with previous month.

### Year-to-Date (YTD)
Aggregates values from start of year to current date.

---

## 🎯 Performance Measures

### Actual vs Target Variance
Difference between actual performance and business targets.

### Achievement %
(Actual / Target) * 100

---

## ⚙️ Design Principles

- Measures are reusable across all dashboards  
- Centralized KPI logic ensures consistency  
- Optimized for performance in large datasets  
- Built using standard DAX best practices  

---

## 📌 Business Value

These measures enable consistent, scalable, and reliable KPI tracking across all business functions including Finance, Sales, Marketing, Supply Chain, and Executive reporting.
